Harshini J Anand is a postgraduate in Neuroscience, looking to pursue her PhD in Cognitive Neuroscience.  She has done her BSc. in Zoology from Savitribhai Phule University, Pune, and her Master’s in Neuroscience from Jiwaji University, Gwalior.

Her keen interest in Music and Classical Shlokhas has led her to  collaborate with reputed scientists in the field of the likes of Dr. Deepti Navaratna to understand the effect of Sanskrit enunciation on the memory of children.
